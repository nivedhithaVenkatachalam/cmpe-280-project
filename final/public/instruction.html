Game Instructions

Frogger Ninja! Its not what it seems. To reach home the frogs would have to make their way from dangerous highways full of fast vehicles. Then make their way from high dense trees. But wait, its not that easy. In random spots the frog may hop onto trick or treats. He could get a boost and be a rocket or get a flash and teleport to short distance. As you are ninjas you can obstruct the enemy by placing a death wall infront of him, or you may teleport himself just behind you. But beware you have only three lives. So hurry and reach home. At the end only one frog can make it!

GAME OBJECTIVE


The Objective of the game is to hop as fast as you can. Gain power ups by stepping in trick or treat. Slow you enemies. You can place a wall infront of him or make lot of trees to slow him. Plan accordingly and reach to sea before the other frog does.

CONTROLS


Action Key
Forward W
Backward S
Right D
Left A
Place a Tree T
Place a Wall L

END OF GAME


Game ends when you either reach to sea first or you die three times.
